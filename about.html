Our Story

Founded in 2010, AutoFix began as a small garage with a big dream - to provide honest, reliable auto repair services to our community. What started as a one-mechanic shop has grown into a full-service auto repair center with a team of certified technicians.

We believe in treating every customer like family and every vehicle like our own. Our commitment to quality service and fair pricing has earned us the trust of thousands of satisfied customers over the years.
